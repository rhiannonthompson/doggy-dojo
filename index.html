About Doggy Dojo

Have you ever wondered what your dog is thinking when he stares adoringly into your eyes? Do you think he is contemplating his unremitting love for squeaky chickens? Or is he planning when to jam his muzzle into the compost bin? Maybe, he is listening for the perilous word, “walkies”.

Well, you are wrong! Your dog is actually thinking, “you stupid biped! Why can you not see, I AM NINJA!”
